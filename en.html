FAQ
Why does the member counter show more members on the invitations than on the server counter?

The server counter does not count bots and secondary accounts, unlike the Discord counter which includes all accounts

What is the difference between the roles?

Each of these 5 roles have a Solo and Loop category but you can't cumulate two roles of the same category
(ex: World Class - Solo and Master - Solo)

World Class : Participation in an international event on stage
Top 40 Solo / Top 16 Loop FR : Participation in the French Beatbox Championship on stage
Master : Get a very good ranking at an online event (Top 3 FBHB, Top 40 FrancoMicroPhonie, international participation)
Skilled : Get a correct ranking at an online event (Top 8 FBHB or equivalent)
Battler : Participation in some Friendly Battles or participation in an event with a wildcard or an elimination

Staff and members with a reputation role can give reputation points to other members. When a member reaches 10 reputation points, he gets the Friendly role, and for 20 reputation points, he gets the Trusted role. These roles give access to a channel where you can directly discuss with the Staff to propose your ideas or host future events if you want. In addition, the Trusted role allows you to participate in meetings with the Staff

Why was my message automatically deleted in #suggestions?

For any suggestion proposed in this channel, the length of your message must exceed 150 characters. This forces you to develop and be precise, and it avoids that your message is drowned after the reactions like "Your idea is great!"
